Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions about Cleanify vacuum solutions.

What makes Cleanify vacuums unique?

Cleanify vacuums are equipped with advanced suction technology and HEPA filtration, ensuring deep cleaning and better air quality.

How do I maintain my Cleanify vacuum?

Regularly clean the filters, empty the dustbin, and check for blockages in the hose to ensure optimal performance.

Do Cleanify vacuums come with a warranty?

Yes, all Cleanify products come with a 2-year warranty. Please refer to the warranty terms for more details.

Can I use Cleanify vacuums on all floor types?

Absolutely! Cleanify vacuums are designed to work efficiently on carpets, hardwood floors, and tiles.

How can I order replacement parts?

You can order replacement parts directly from our website or contact our customer support team for assistance.
