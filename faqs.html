Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What is Cleanify?

A: Cleanify is a company dedicated to providing high-quality, smart vacuum cleaners designed to make cleaning easy and efficient.

Q: Where can I purchase Cleanify products?

A: You can purchase Cleanify products directly from our website under the Products page or at select retail stores.

Product Questions

Q: What types of vacuum cleaners does Cleanify offer?

A: Cleanify offers a range of vacuum cleaners including the Cleanify Pro, Cleanify Mini, and Cleanify Robot, each designed for different cleaning needs and preferences.

Q: How do I maintain my Cleanify vacuum cleaner?

A: Regular maintenance includes emptying the dustbin, cleaning the filters, and checking the brushes for debris. Detailed instructions can be found in the user manual that comes with your product.

Warranty & Support

Q: What warranty do Cleanify products come with?

A: All Cleanify vacuum cleaners come with a one-year limited warranty. Please refer to the warranty documentation included with your product for more details.

Q: How can I contact customer support?

A: You can contact our support team via email at support@cleanify.com or by phone at (814) 555-9608. Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.

Shipping & Returns

Q: How long does shipping take?

A: Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping options are also available at checkout.

Q: What is Cleanify's return policy?

A: We offer a 30-day return policy on all our products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our support team to initiate a return.
